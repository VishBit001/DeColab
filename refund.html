Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: August 6, 2025

1. Coin Purchases

All purchases of virtual coins within the app are final and non-refundable. Users are advised to review their wallet balance and purchase limits before initiating any transaction.

2. Exceptions

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • Duplicate transactions due to technical errors
  • Unauthorized charges proven through investigation
  • Failure to deliver purchased coins due to system malfunction

3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, users must email vishesh39.verma@gmail.com within 7 days of the transaction, providing:

  • Transaction ID
  • Registered email or phone number
  • Reason for the refund request

We will review and respond to refund requests within 5 business days.

4. Cancellations

Users may cancel their account at any time by contacting support. However, cancellation does not entitle users to refunds for previously purchased coins.

5. Gateway Fees

In cases where refunds are approved, any applicable payment gateway processing fees may be deducted from the refund amount.

6. Legal Compliance

This policy complies with applicable Indian laws governing digital transactions and consumer protection. We reserve the right to update this policy at any time.

7. Contact

For any questions or concerns, please contact us at vishesh39.verma@gmail.com.
